about

Hi !

I'm Jonathan Dobson !

I'm a software developer

My passion for programming software began during grade school math class when students were introduced to the TI-83 graphing calculator, an amazing machine which included with purchase, a comprehensive user manual along with it's very own built-in scripting language.

It was at this point, I took interest in the amazing power and the unlimited possibilities offered by computers and software. I spent most of my free time in high school building apps for the calculator, all while continuing to gain knowlege and skill that would take me to college. During college, I explored software and discovered and identified the greatness of web-based apps.
